Questions? 🤔

 

What ages is it for? · เหมาะกับอายุเท่าไร

Designed for children aged 0–5, with an adult nearby. Big buttons and friendly voices suit tiny hands and ears.

Do I need a camera or a printer?

No. The main experience plays entirely on screen. AR (camera + printed marker) is an optional bonus for older kids.

Does it work offline?

Yes — after the first load it can be installed like an app and played without internet.

Which devices work best?

Any modern phone or tablet. Spoken English is clearest on Chrome, Edge, and Android.
